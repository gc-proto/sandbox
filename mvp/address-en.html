Change of address

Each department in the Government of Canada has a separate record of your address when you register with them. To update the Government of Canada about a change of address, you must:

  • Contact each department individually to change each record.

Most commonly contacted departments

  • Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada
  • Canada Revenue Agency (businesses)
  • Canada Revenue Agency (individuals)
  • Service Canada (EI, CPP, OAS)
